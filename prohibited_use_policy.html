Privacy Information

AI Power Grid operates using a distributed network of worker nodes that process user requests. Please be aware of the following privacy considerations:

  • Your prompts, inputs, and generated outputs are processed by distributed worker nodes in our network.
  • While we implement security practices, the distributed nature of our service means that worker nodes temporarily have access to the content being processed.
  • We recommend not including sensitive personal information, confidential data, or private details in your prompts or requests.
  • For highly sensitive use cases, consider using dedicated, private AI solutions rather than public distributed services.
  • By using our service, you acknowledge and accept that your inputs will be processed by our distributed worker network.

We value your privacy and implement reasonable measures to protect your information, but the inherent architecture of distributed AI processing systems involves multiple processing points. Our worker node operators are expected to maintain appropriate security and privacy practices, but we cannot guarantee end-to-end privacy of all inputs processed through our distributed network.
